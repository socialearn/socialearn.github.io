3. Buatlah procedure text tentang how to send SMS?


HOW TO SEND A SMS MESSAGE SMS, or Short Messaging Service, messages are commonly referred to as text messages. The act of sending an SMS is called "texting." To send a text message, or SMS, from a cell phone may be straightforward or may be a bit complex, depending on the cell phone model. Things you need : Sms-enable cell phone. Instruction : Clik the "Message" button, or click Application > Message > Create Message. Enter the phone number or email address of the SMS recipient. Type a SMS message. SMS message are typically 160 character or less. If you send many characters, you send a speech :D Click "Send" to send your SMS Verify that your message was sent by checking your sent items box. If the message is waiting in the Sent items, it means your messages are not read yet
