3. Please mention chemical using for dishwash


LABS (Linear Alkyl Benzene Sulfonate).

This material is a core material that must be included in the Dishwashing Liquid formula. The physical form of this material is a light brown color, rather sticky and slippery in the hand. The LABS chemical structure is simpler than DDBS. But that is precisely the advantage because this LABS is easily broken down by the soil which in other words is categorized as environmentally friendly material.

Caustic (NaOH).

The original form of caustic is a small, thin plate commonly called flake. In the process of making Dishwashing Chilers, Caustic must be dissolved in water first. The comparison between caustic and water is 40:60. Example: when 100g of caustic solution will be made, 40g of Caustic flake is dissolved in 60 cc of water. Likewise, if you want to make a 1kg Kaoustic solution, then 400 g of flake is dissolved in 600cc of water. And so on when making a large amount of Caustic solution, use the same comparison. In making a caustic solution, the factor of safety (safety) needs to be considered properly considering that this material is quite hard. The containers used also do not use metal materials because they will dissolve.

Emal-70.

Is a clear paste-shaped liquid. Function to add foam and give a soft impression in the hand. The problem is that the price of this material is quite expensive.

Atinsoft solution.

This material is not an ingredient that we make ourselves by mixing water and caustic solutions with a certain ratio.

Salt.

The provision of salt in the process is primarily intended to increase product viscosity. However, the presence of salt will slightly reduce the clarity of the product.

Pigment.

Commonly used dyes on Dishwashing Liquid products are green and yellow. Even so, you can develop products with more varied colors.

Perfume.

Commonly used perfumes are oranges. Why are the citrus scents that consumers like most? This is due to the function of the citrus aroma which can 'repel' the smell of food residue that is attached to the plate dominantly. But other scents can also be used.
