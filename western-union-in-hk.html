17. hiii! i need help! Pacific Daylight Time (UTC−7), daylight saving time in western North America.If its 6 pm in western North Americawhat time is it in Java (indonesia) ​


Jawaban:

8 am in Java

Penjelasan:

Java uses UTC+7 without any daylight saving time. Most of the Southeast Asian countries never use daylight saving time due to its location which is close to the equator.

Like other north European countries, the Pacific area of the US and Canada (LA-Vancouver)-on the other hand-is now on the Daylight Time until November 7th 2021. Thus, the area uses UTC-7

Therefore the time difference would be:

7 + 7 hours =

14 hours

The Pacific coast area of the US and Canada is 14 hours behind Java.

6 pm + 14 hours = 20

20 - 12 hrs = 8 am

8 am in Java
