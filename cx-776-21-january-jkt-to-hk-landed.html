1. Tgs : membuat kalimat +.-.? Contoh : I went to jkt yesterday. I din not go to jkt yesterday. Did you go to jkt yesterday?10 kalimat dengan kt kerja yg berbeda​


Membuat 10 kalimat Positif (+), Negatif (-), dan Interogatif (?) dengan kata kerja yang berbeda adalah sebagai berikut:

(+) She wanted to go to supermarket yesterday.
(-) She did not want to go to supermarket yesterday.
(?) Did she want to go to supermarket yesterday?(+) He planned his summer holiday last week.
(-) He did not plan his summer holiday last week.
(?) Did he plan his summer holiday last week?(+) When I gave her a drink, she stopped crying.
(-) If I didn’t give her a drink, she would cry?
(?) Why did you give her a drink?(+) My Math class began at 9.30.
(-) My Math class did not begin at 8.00.
(?) Did your Math class begin at 9.00?(+) I ate my dinner two hour ago.
(-) I did not eat dinner with friends.
(?) Did you eat dinner with friends?(+) My cat drank milk two days ago.
(-) My cat didn’t drink milk yesterday.
(?) Did your cat drink chocolate milk yesterday?(+) She liked the ice cream in a food stall like this.
(-) She didn’t like the ice cream in an expensive restaurant.
(?) Did she like the ice cream in an expensive restaurant?(+) It was my mistaken.
(-) It wasn’t my mistake.
(?) Was it your mistake?(+) He was my first crush.
(-) He wasn’t my first crush.
(?) Was he your first crush?(+) Sussy was sick last week.
(-) Sussy wasn’t sick last week.
(?) Was Sussy sick last week?

Simple past tense adalah bentuk kata sederhana yang mewakili peristiwa yang terjadi di masa lalu. Kata kerja yang digunakan dalam tense ini adalah kata kerja kedua (verb 2). Bentuk ini adalah salah satu tenses paling dasar dan sering digunakan saat menulis atau berbicara bahasa Inggris. Dalam suatu situasi, terkadang seseorang perlu menceritakan suatu peristiwa yang terjadi di masa lalu, oleh karena itu penting untuk mempelajari kalimat simple past tense.

Rumus Simple Past Tense Positive

Subject + Verb 2 + Object

Rumus Simple Past Tense Negative

Subject + did + not + verb 1

Rumus Simple Past Tense Interrogative

Did + subject + verb 1
