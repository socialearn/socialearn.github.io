21. i......to go to swimming pool but she​


There are several probable answers to complete the sentence above, especially if there is no limit to the number of words. The most obvious one is possibly this one. (Ada sejumlah pilihan jawaban yang bisa digunakan. Yang paling umum adalah dibawah ini)

I want to go to the swimming pool but she prefers somewhere else.

That shows a preference for it. Yet, one thing is for sure, there should be a contrastive statement at the end of the main clause since there is a connector 'but'. ( Kalimat diatas menunjukkan preferensi. Meski begitu tetap harus disertakan pernyataan kontras mengingat ada kata hubung 'but')

Penjelasan:

Here are some other possible answers which can be considered.

I would like to go to the swimming pool but she hates it. (showing the interest in the word 'would like' and 'hate')I had to go to the swimming pool but she prevented me from going there.  (showing the expression of necessity with the word 'had to' (the past form of 'have to')I was supposed to go to the swimming pool but she lured me to stay with her at home for the rest of the evening. (showing the expression of obligation with the words 'supposed to'I am bound to go to the swimming pool but she might reject the idea of me going due to the possibility of contracting COVID-19. (showing the expression of possibility with the words 'am bound to' which has the same meaning as 'will definitely)I am obliged to go to the swimming pool but she is not in favour of granting her permission to go since she has already had a premonition of me being seduced by the old flame of mine. (showing the formal version of expression of obligation with the words 'obliged to')

So there are practically various ways of completing the sentence above.
