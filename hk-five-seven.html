= 6 susunan

SEVEN

Jumlah huruf = 5

Huruf ganda = E = 2

= Jumlah huruf! / Huruf ganda!

= 5! / 2!

= 5 × 4 × 3 × 2! / 2!

= 5 × 4 × 3

= 60 susunan

FIVE

F = 1 I = 1 V = 1 E = 1

Jumlah huruf = 4

Huruf ganda = -

= Jumlah huruf!

= 4!

= 4 × 3 × 2 × 1

= 24 susunan

= 5 × 4 × 3

= 60 susunan
