20. Raj buys 200 boxes of apples at $28 per box. There are 60 apples in each box. 15% of the apples are rotten. Find the selling price per apple if he wants to earn an 80% profit at cost price.


≈harga beli awal (modal) = 200 x $28 = $5600
1 kotak isi 60 apel, maka 200 kotak = 200 x 60 = 12000 apel
15% apel sudah rusak/busuk, berarti 15% x 12000 = 1800 apel
sisa apel yang masih bisa di jual 12000 - 1800 = 10200

keuntungan yang diharapkan 80% dari harga beli = 80% x 5600 = $4480

harga jual = 5600 + 4480 = $10080

harga jual per apel nya menjadi = 10080 / 10200 ≈ $0,99
