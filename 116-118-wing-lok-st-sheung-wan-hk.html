Conversation 5

Conversation 6

Conversation 8

Riri: We have made handicrafts and sold them.

Conversation 9

Riri: We have given thirty street children twenty thousand rupiahs each.

Riri: We have given each family a gift containing sugar, cooking oil, and rice.

Untuk menjawab soal-soal ini, siswa diminta untuk memperhatikan penggunaan present perfect tense pada kalimat positif dan kalimat tanya.
