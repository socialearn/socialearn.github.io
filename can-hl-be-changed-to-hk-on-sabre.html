A form of energy that can be easily changed to other forms is called electrical energy. An example is an electric lamp -- the electrical energy is changed to heat or light.

Explanation

Electrical energy is one of energy form that can be easily changed to be other forms. Actually, every energy can change from one form to another, but changes in some energies actually require more energy and make energy dissipated. Here are the reasons why electrical energy is often used to be converted into other forms of energy to fulfill the needs of human life :

The energy level in electrical energy is high because electrical energy contain large entropy. Electricity can be transferred easily via electric lines over distances and it is not affected by changing of temperature (hot/ cold).Resistances of the transportation of electric energy may be reduced with conductors, so the losses can be minimize in transportation and distribution of electrical power.Electrical energy can be converted to other form of energy with up to 100% efficiency (high efficiency) because of the high grade of electrical energy (first reason).The majority of devices in households is electrical equipment. Thus, electrical energy supply to residential houses is an indispensable needs.The use of various types of energy sources as inputs will make human life more complicated. Thus, electrical energy play a role as universal comparison bases for different machines.

Conclusion

The main reason of electrical energy can be converted easily to be other forms : electrical energy is a high grade energy that will have high efficiency when it to be converted into lower grade energy. All other forms like heat energy, gravitational energy, mechanical energy, and light energy are low grade and are not recommended for end users.

26. There are too much cars on the street. The word much is false. So it can be changed by.


Kalimat pada osal diatas mengandung Quantifier untuk countable nouns (benda yang dapat dihitung). Sehingga quantifier yang tepat digunakan adalah:

There are too many cars on the street

Pembahasan

Jadi, countable quantifiers adalah kata pembilang untuk menyatakan kuantitas atau jumlah dari kata benda (noun) yang dapat dihitung. Countable noun contohnya seperti chair, table, apple, glasses, dan lain-lain. Contoh quantifier yang dapat digunakan: many, few, large amount, etc

Uncountable quantifiers adalah kata atau frasa yang berlaku saat kita ingin menunjukkan jumlah dari noun yang tak dapat dihitung. Contoh uncountable noun adalah milk, water, sand, grass, rice, dan masih banyak lagi. Contoh quantifier yang dapat digunakan: much, little, a bit, etc
