8. to enter an afair, 1 adult ticket costs $10 and 1 child ticket cost $6. The ratio of the number of adult ticket sold to the number of child sold was 6:5. A total of $8100 was collected from the sale of all tickets. How many adult tickets were sold?


1 adult ticket : $10

1 child ticket : $6

adult ticket sold : child ticket sold = 6 : 5

adult ticket sold + child ticket sold = $8100

adult ticket sold : ?

$8100 × 5/6 = $6750

$6750 : $10 = 675

Answer : 675 tickets
