[Telling time]

it is six o' clock.it is a quarter past six.it is half past six.it is a quarter to six.it is seven o' clock.it is a quarter past twelve.it is twenty to one.it is half past one.it is a quarter past seven.it is a quarter to ten.

PEMBAHASAN

Telling time adalah cara untuk menyebut jam yang digunakan di negara bagian barat.

Terbagi menjadi dua cara yaitu:

1. American English

Pada bahasa Inggris yang digunakan oleh warga Amerika, yang disebut dahulu adalah jamnya, baru menitnya.

contoh:

09:15 = it is nine fifteen. (jam sembilan lima belas)09:50 = it is nine fifty. (jam sembilan lima puluh)

2. British English

Pada bahasa Inggris yang digunakan oleh warga Britania Raya, yang disebut dahulu adalah menitnya, baru jamnya.

contoh:

02:02 = it is two past two. (dua menit melewati jam dua)02:58 = it is two to two. (dua menit menuju jam dua)

↪so, the answer:

1. 06:00 =

(BE & AE) it is six o' clock.

2. 06:15 =

(BE) it is a quarter past six.(AE) it is six fifteen.

3. 06:30 =

(BE) it is half past six.(AE) it is six thirty.

4. 06:45 =

(BE) it is a quarter to six.(AE) it is six forty-five.

5. 07:00 =

(BE & AE) it is seven o' clock.

6. 12:15 =

(BE) it is a quarter past twelve.(AE) it is twelve fifteen.

7. 12:40 =

(BE) it is twenty to one.(AE) it is twelve forty.

8. 01:30 =

(BE) it is half past one.(AE) it is one thirty.

9. 07:15 =

(BE) it is a quarter past seven.(AE) it is seven fifteen.

10. 09:45 =

(BE) it is a quarter to ten.(AE) it is nine forty-five.

note:

BE means British English.AE means American English.
